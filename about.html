ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm a multimedia designer student from UCL Erhvervsakademi og professionshøjskole located in Odense. I'm currently studying on my first semester. Nice to meet you!


I care a lot about enhancing the players experience through animations without lowering the performance of the actual game or site.


My specialty lies in the digital art and animations thereof. My software skills primarily include the Adobe programs, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects for drawing and animating my content. I am also proficient in working with Clip Studio Paint, DaVinci Resolve 18, and Visual Studio Code as alternatives to create my work, but I am not scared to learn any new programs or methods, should the need be. Should you ever need a login screen, event panel, background image or simply an animated button or emote etc. I'm the one of the job.


Contact me if you wish to learn more!
