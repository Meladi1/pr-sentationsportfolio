ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm a multimedia designer student from UCL Erhvervsakademi og professionshøjskole located in Odense. I'm currently studying on her first semester. Nice to meet you!


I care a lot about enchancing the players experience through animations without lowering the perforance of the actual game or site.


My specialty lies in the digital art and the animations thereof. My programs primarily include the Adobe programs Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects for and other programs such as Clip Studio Paint, DaVince Resolve 18 and Visual Studio Code to create my work, but I'm not scared to learn something new, should the need me. So should you ever need a login screen, event panel, background image or simply an animated button or emote etc. I'm the one of the job.


Contact me if you wish to learn more!
